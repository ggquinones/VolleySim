Order of Stats

SP	K	E	TA	K%	A	SA	SE	RE	DIGS	BS	BA	BE	BHE	PTS

SP = Sets Played
K = kills
E = hitting error
TA = Total hitting Attempts
K% = Kill Efficiency
A = Assists
SA = Service Ace
SE = Service Error
RE = Reception Error
DIGS = Digs
BS = Block Solo
BA = Block Assists
BE = Blocking Error
BHE = Ball Handling Error
PTS = Points
